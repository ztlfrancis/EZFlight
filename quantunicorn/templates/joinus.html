UI/UX Designer for QuantUnicorn

Please send your inquiry to hr@quantunicorn.com

Quantunicorn is a Fintech Software Development company providing big data analytics and quantitative trading strategies for global equity and derivative trading. 

Responsibilities

  • Gathering and evaluating user requirements, in collaboration with marketing, product and engineering team
  • Illustrating design ideas using storyboards, process flows and sitemaps
  • Designing graphic user interface elements, like menus, tabs and widgets
  • Build page navigation buttons and search fields
  • Develop UI mockups and prototypes that clearly illustrate how sites function and look like
  • Create original graphic designs (e.g. images, sketches and tables)
  • Prepare and present rough drafts to internal teams and key stakeholders
  • Identify and troubleshoot UX problems (e.g. responsiveness)
  • Conduct layout adjustments based on user feedback
  • Adhere to style standards on fonts, colors and images

Requirements

  • Part time position requires good time-management skills
  • Proven work experience as a UI/UX Designer or similar role
  • Portfolio of design projects
  • Up-to-date knowledge of design software like Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop
  • Team spirit; strong communication skills to collaborate with various stakeholders and external clients
  • BSc in Design, Computer Science or relevant field
  • Fluent in both Chinese and English
  • Work virtually with the team, no office location requirement
  • Basic knowledge of financial and stock market
